---
title: Transfer a domain
description: How to transfer an existing domain from another registrar to SpeedyPage.
---

You can transfer a domain from another registrar to SpeedyPage through the client area. Transfers usually complete within 5-7 days.

## Before you start

Your domain must meet these requirements before you can transfer it:

- **Registered for more than 60 days** — ICANN requires domains to be at least 60 days old before transferring
- **Not transferred in the last 60 days** — if you recently transferred the domain to another registrar, you must wait 60 days
- **Unlocked** — the domain must not have a transfer lock enabled
- **You have the EPP/auth code** — this is a unique code from your current registrar that authorizes the transfer

## Prepare your domain at the current registrar

1. Log in to your current registrar's control panel
2. Disable the transfer lock (sometimes called "domain lock" or "registrar lock")
3. Obtain the EPP code (also called an authorization code or auth code) — this is usually available in your registrar's domain management settings or can be requested from their support team

## Transfer your domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the client area">
    Go to [my.speedypage.com](https://my.speedypage.com) and log in with your account credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a domain transfer">
    Click **Domains** → **Transfer a Domain to Us**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your domain">
    Type the domain name you want to transfer and click **Search**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the EPP code">
    Enter the EPP/authorization code from your current registrar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Review your order and complete the checkout process. Domain transfers include a one-year renewal added to your current expiry date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
After you complete checkout, your current registrar will send a confirmation email to the domain's admin contact. You may need to approve the transfer through that email. If the transfer is not rejected, it completes automatically within 5-7 days.
</Note>

## After the transfer

Once the transfer completes:

- Your domain is managed through the SpeedyPage client area
- Nameservers are preserved from the previous registrar — update them to `ns1.speedy.page` and `ns2.speedy.page` if you want to use SpeedyPage hosting. See [Anycast nameservers](/web-hosting/nameservers) for details.
- DNS records continue to resolve as before until you make changes
