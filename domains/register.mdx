title: Register a domain
description: How to register a new domain name through the SpeedyPage client area.
---

You can register a domain directly through the SpeedyPage client area. Domains registered with SpeedyPage are automatically configured with SpeedyPage nameservers.

## Register a new domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the client area">
    Go to [my.speedypage.com](https://my.speedypage.com) and log in with your account credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a domain search">
    Click **Domains** → **Register a New Domain**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for your domain">
    Enter the domain name you want and click **Search**. You'll see a list of available extensions and their prices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your domain">
    Choose the domain and extension you want, then click **Add to Cart**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure nameservers">
    Choose your nameserver settings. The default nameservers are:

    - `ns1.speedy.page`
    - `ns2.speedy.page`

    Keep these defaults if you're using SpeedyPage hosting. If you're using an external DNS provider, enter their nameservers instead.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Review your order and complete the checkout process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your domain is active immediately after registration. If you're using SpeedyPage nameservers, DNS records are managed through cPanel's Zone Editor. See [Manage DNS records](/domains/dns-records) for details.

<Tip>
Annual hosting plans include a free domain registration. If you have an eligible plan, the domain discount is applied automatically at checkout.
</Tip>
