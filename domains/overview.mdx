SpeedyPage offers domain registration, transfers, and DNS management — all handled through the [client area](https://my.speedypage.com). You can register a new domain, transfer an existing one from another registrar, or manage DNS records for domains on your hosting plan.

## Nameservers

If you're using SpeedyPage hosting, point your domain to:

| Nameserver | Hostname |
| --- | --- |
| Primary | `ns1.speedy.page` |
| Secondary | `ns2.speedy.page` |

For more details, see [Anycast nameservers](/web-hosting/nameservers).
