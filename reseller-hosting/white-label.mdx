title: White-label branding

---

SpeedyPage Reseller Hosting is fully white-label. Your clients see your brand throughout cPanel — not SpeedyPage. This lets you offer hosting under your own business name.

## What white-label means

When a client logs in to cPanel, they see:

- Your company name and logo
- Your custom branding and color scheme
- No reference to SpeedyPage

Your clients interact with your brand only. SpeedyPage operates as the infrastructure behind the scenes.

## Customize cPanel branding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to WHM">
    Access WHM through your [client area](https://my.speedypage.com) or by visiting `yourdomain.com/whm`. See [Getting started with WHM](/reseller-hosting/getting-started) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open cPanel Customization">
    In WHM, go to **cPanel** → **Customization**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your branding">
    Customize the following:

    - **Company name** — displayed in the cPanel header and footer
    - **Logo** — your company logo shown in the cPanel interface
    - **Documentation URL** — link to your own help documentation (optional)
    - **Support URL** — link to your own support portal (optional)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save** to apply the branding. Changes take effect immediately for all client cPanel accounts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use your own domain for WHM and cPanel access

By default, clients access cPanel through your primary reseller domain. If you want clients to access cPanel through a branded URL (e.g., `cpanel.yourbrand.com`), you can set this up using DNS.

Create a CNAME or A record pointing your custom hostname to your reseller server. Your clients can then access cPanel at `yourbrand.com/cpanel` instead of the default hostname.

## Set up custom nameservers

By default, your clients' domains use SpeedyPage's nameservers (`ns1.speedy.page` and `ns2.speedy.page`). For full white-label branding, you can use custom nameservers under your own domain (e.g., `ns1.yourbrand.com` and `ns2.yourbrand.com`).

<Note>
Custom nameservers require DNS configuration on SpeedyPage's side. [Contact support](https://speedypage.com/contact) to set up custom nameservers for your reseller account.
</Note>
