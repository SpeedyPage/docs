WHM (Web Host Manager) is the reseller control panel. You use it to create and manage client cPanel accounts, configure hosting packages, and handle DNS and email settings.

## Access WHM

You can access WHM in two ways.

### Through the client area

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the client area">
    Go to [my.speedypage.com](https://my.speedypage.com) and log in with your account credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your reseller service">
    Click **Services** and select your Reseller Hosting plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in to WHM">
    Click the **Log in to WHM** button. This opens WHM in a new tab without requiring a separate username and password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Direct URL

You can access WHM directly by visiting:

```text
yourdomain.com/whm
```

Replace `yourdomain.com` with your actual domain name. Log in with the WHM username and password from your welcome email.

## Key areas in WHM

### Account Functions

This is where you create and manage your clients' cPanel accounts. Key options:

- **Create a New Account** — set up a new cPanel account for a client
- **List Accounts** — view all accounts on your reseller plan
- **Suspend/Unsuspend an Account** — temporarily disable or re-enable access
- **Terminate an Account** — permanently remove a client account

### Packages

Packages define the resource limits and features for client accounts. Before creating client accounts, set up your hosting packages:

- **Add a Package** — define storage, bandwidth, email accounts, and other limits
- **Edit a Package** — modify an existing package's resources
- **Delete a Package** — remove a package you no longer need

<Tip>
Create your hosting packages before creating client accounts. When you create an account, you assign it a package that determines its resource limits.
</Tip>

### DNS Functions

Manage DNS zones for domains on your reseller account:

- **Add a DNS Zone** — create DNS records for a new domain
- **Edit a DNS Zone** — modify existing DNS records
- **Delete a DNS Zone** — remove a domain's DNS records

### Email

Configure email settings for accounts on your reseller plan, including email routing and authentication records.

## Plan limits

Each Reseller Hosting tier has different account and storage limits:

| Plan | cPanel accounts | NVMe SSD storage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | 25 | 40 GB |
| Premium | 50 | 100 GB |
| Enterprise | 100 | 250 GB |

<Note>
These limits apply to your entire reseller account. You control how resources are distributed across your clients through the packages you create in WHM.
</Note>
