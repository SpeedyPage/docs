---
title: Enable canonical links on BunnyCDN
description: Add canonical headers to your BunnyCDN pull zone to prevent duplicate content in search engine results.
---

A canonical link tells search engines which URL is the primary version of your page. Enabling canonical headers on BunnyCDN prevents CDN URLs from being indexed as duplicate content.

## Enable canonical headers

1. Log in to your [client area](https://my.speedypage.com) and go to **Services**, then select your hosting plan
2. Open **CDN Configuration** from the left sidebar
3. Click **Manage Zone**
4. Open the **Headers** menu
5. Turn on **Add Canonical Headers**
6. Click **Save CORS Settings** to apply

Your CDN pull zone will now include canonical headers, directing search engines to your primary domain.
