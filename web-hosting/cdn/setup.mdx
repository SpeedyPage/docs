---
title: Set up BunnyCDN
description: Activate and configure BunnyCDN on your SpeedyPage hosting plan for faster global content delivery.
---

SpeedyPage includes BunnyCDN, Bunny Shield, and Bunny Optimizer on selected hosting plans. Once activated, your site's static content is cached and served from Bunny's global network for faster load times.

## Activate the CDN

1. Log in to cPanel through your [client area](https://my.speedypage.com) or by visiting `yourdomain.com/cpanel`
2. Go to the **Domains** section and find the **bunny.net** icon
3. Select your domain and click **Set Up With bunny.net**

## Update your nameservers

After activating BunnyCDN, update your domain's nameservers at your registrar to:

| Nameserver | Hostname |
| --- | --- |
| Primary | `ns1.speedy.page` |
| Secondary | `ns2.speedy.page` |

Click **Finish Setup** once the nameservers are updated.

<Note>
DNS changes may take some time to propagate globally, but your site will remain online during this process.
</Note>

## Configure CDN settings

The default configuration works for most sites with no changes needed. If you need to adjust CDN settings or purge the cache, you can do so through the **bunny.net** section in cPanel.
