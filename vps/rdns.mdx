Reverse DNS (rDNS) maps an IP address back to a hostname — the opposite of a standard DNS lookup. rDNS is commonly required for mail servers, as many receiving servers reject email from IPs without valid reverse DNS.

## Prerequisites

Before setting rDNS, you must create a forward DNS record (A or AAAA) pointing your hostname to the server's IP address. rDNS will not work without a matching forward record.

## Set up rDNS

1. Log in to your [client area](https://my.speedypage.com) and open your VPS service
2. Click **Manage** on the server you want to configure
3. Go to the **Network** tab
4. Find your IP address in the list and click **Reverse DNS**
5. Enter the hostname you want the IP to resolve to
6. Click **Update** to save
