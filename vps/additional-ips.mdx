---
title: Additional IP addresses
description: How to add extra IP addresses to your SpeedyPage VPS and what to expect.
---

You can add extra IPv4 addresses to your VPS at **$1.99/month per IP**. Each request is reviewed individually and requires a justification for the additional addresses.

## Request additional IPs

Open a support ticket through your [client area](https://my.speedypage.com) with the subject **Additional IP Request** and include:

- Your current VPS IP address
- The number of additional IPs you need
- The intended use for each address

## What to know

- Additional IPs are assigned individually, not as contiguous blocks. If you need a specific subnet range, mention this in your ticket and the team will explore options.
- Regional or usage-based limits may apply.
- To remove an extra IP, contact support. A server restart is required to apply the change.
