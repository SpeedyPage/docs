---
title: Available operating systems
description: Operating systems available for SpeedyPage VPS servers, including Linux distributions, FreeBSD, and Windows Server.

SpeedyPage VPS servers support a wide range of operating systems. You can select your OS during provisioning or reinstall at any time from the VirtFusion control panel.
