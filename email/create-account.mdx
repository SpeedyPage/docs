---
title: Create an email account
description: How to create an email account in cPanel on your SpeedyPage hosting plan.
---

All SpeedyPage hosting plans include unlimited email accounts. You create and manage them through cPanel.

## Create an email account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Access cPanel through your [client area](https://my.speedypage.com) or by visiting `yourdomain.com/cpanel`. See [Access cPanel](/getting-started/cpanel) for detailed instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Email Accounts">
    In the **Email** section, click **Email Accounts**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the account">
    Click **Create** and fill in the following:

    - **Username** — the part before the @ symbol (e.g., `info` for info@yourdomain.com)
    - **Domain** — select the domain from the dropdown if you have more than one
    - **Password** — set a strong password or use the generator
    - **Storage space** — set a mailbox quota or leave as unlimited
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create">
    Click **Create** to finish. The email account is ready to use immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
After creating an account, you can access email through [webmail](/email/webmail) or configure a [desktop or mobile email client](/email/client-setup).
</Tip>

## Manage existing accounts

From the **Email Accounts** page in cPanel, you can:

- Change passwords
- Adjust mailbox quotas
- Suspend or delete accounts
- Access webmail directly via the **Check Email** button
