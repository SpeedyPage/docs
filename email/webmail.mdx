Webmail lets you read and send email from your browser without configuring a desktop or mobile client. SpeedyPage includes two webmail clients: Roundcube and Horde.

## Access webmail

You can access webmail in two ways:

### Through your domain

Visit the following URL in your browser, replacing `yourdomain.com` with your actual domain:

```text
yourdomain.com/webmail
```

Log in with your full email address and email account password.

### Through cPanel

1. Log in to cPanel through your [client area](https://my.speedypage.com) or by visiting `yourdomain.com/cpanel`
2. Go to the **Email** section and click **Email Accounts**
3. Click **Check Email** next to the account you want to access
4. Select a webmail client to open your inbox

## Choose a webmail client

| Client | Best for |
| --- | --- |
| **Roundcube** | Most users — clean interface, straightforward to use |
| **Horde** | Users who need calendar and task management features |

<Tip>
Roundcube is recommended for most users. It provides a modern interface and handles everyday email tasks well.
</Tip>
