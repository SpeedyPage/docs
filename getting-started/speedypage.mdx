Welcome to SpeedyPage. This guide walks you through your first steps as a new customer — from signing up to managing your hosting services.

## Create your account

1. Visit [speedypage.com](https://speedypage.com) and choose a hosting plan
2. Complete the order form with your details
3. After payment, you'll receive a welcome email with your login credentials

## Navigate the client area

The client area at [my.speedypage.com](https://my.speedypage.com) is where you manage everything — services, billing, support tickets, and account settings.

After logging in, you'll see:

- **Services** — your active hosting plans, VPS servers, and other products
- **Domains** — domain registrations and DNS management
- **Billing** — invoices, payment methods, and order history
- **Support** — open and track support tickets

## Access your control panel

Your control panel depends on the product you purchased:

| Product | Control panel | How to access |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Web Hosting | cPanel | **Services** → select your plan → **Log in to cPanel** |
| WordPress Hosting | cPanel | **Services** → select your plan → **Log in to cPanel** |
| Reseller Hosting | cPanel | **Services** → select your plan → **Log in to cPanel** |
| Virtual Servers | VirtFusion | **Services** → select your server → **Manage** |

<Tip>
You can also access cPanel directly by visiting `yourdomain.com/cpanel` in your browser.
</Tip>

## Point your domain to SpeedyPage

If your domain is registered elsewhere, update its nameservers to:

| Nameserver | Hostname |
| --- | --- |
| Primary | `ns1.speedy.page` |
| Secondary | `ns2.speedy.page` |

See [Anycast nameservers](/web-hosting/nameservers) for more details.

## Get help

SpeedyPage provides 24/7 UK-based support. If you need assistance:

- **Support tickets** — log in to [my.speedypage.com](https://my.speedypage.com) and open a ticket under **Support**
- **Contact page** — visit [speedypage.com/contact](https://speedypage.com/contact) for all contact options
