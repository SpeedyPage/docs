---
title: Free migrations
description: How to request a free same-day migration from your current cPanel host to SpeedyPage.
---

SpeedyPage offers free same-day migrations from cPanel-based hosts. The support team handles the entire process so you can move to SpeedyPage with minimal effort.

## What gets migrated

- Website files and directories
- MySQL and PostgreSQL databases
- Email accounts and mailbox contents
- cPanel settings and configurations
- Cron jobs

<Note>
Free migrations are available from cPanel-based hosts only. If your current host uses a different control panel, contact support to discuss your options.
</Note>

## Request a migration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a support ticket">
    Log in to [my.speedypage.com](https://my.speedypage.com) and open a support ticket requesting a migration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide your current hosting details">
    Include the following information from your current host:

    - cPanel URL (e.g., `yourdomain.com/cpanel`)
    - cPanel username
    - cPanel password
    - List of domains to migrate (if you have more than one)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for completion">
    The support team will begin your migration and notify you when it's complete. Most migrations are completed the same day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your site">
    Before switching DNS, preview your site on SpeedyPage to confirm everything transferred correctly. The support team can provide a temporary URL for testing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your nameservers">
    Once you've verified everything, update your domain's nameservers to point to SpeedyPage:

    | Nameserver | Hostname |
    | --- | --- |
    | Primary | `ns1.speedy.page` |
    | Secondary | `ns2.speedy.page` |
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Do not update your nameservers until the migration is complete and you've verified your site. Switching DNS early will cause downtime.
</Warning>

## Timeline

Most migrations are completed within the same business day. Larger accounts with many domains or large databases may take longer. The support team will keep you updated throughout the process.
