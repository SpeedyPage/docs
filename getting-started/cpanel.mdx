---
title: Access cPanel
description: How to log in to cPanel through the SpeedyPage client area or directly via your domain.
---

cPanel is the control panel for Web Hosting, WordPress Hosting, and Reseller Hosting (individual accounts). You can access it in two ways.

## Through the client area

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the client area">
    Go to [my.speedypage.com](https://my.speedypage.com) and log in with your account credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your service">
    Click **Services** and select the hosting plan you want to manage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Click the **Log in to cPanel** button. This opens cPanel in a new tab without requiring a separate username and password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Direct URL

You can access cPanel directly by visiting:

```text
yourdomain.com/cpanel
```

Replace `yourdomain.com` with your actual domain name. Log in with the cPanel username and password from your welcome email.

<Note>
If you've changed your cPanel password and forgotten it, you can reset it through the client area by navigating to **Services** → your plan → **Change Password**.
</Note>
