SpeedyPage accepts multiple payment methods and offers flexible billing cycles for all services.

## Accepted payment methods

- **Credit and debit cards** — Visa, Mastercard, and American Express
- **PayPal** — pay directly from your PayPal balance or linked bank account

## Billing cycles

SpeedyPage offers two billing cycles:

| Cycle | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Monthly | Pay each month, cancel any time |
| Annual | Pay for 12 months upfront — includes 2 months free and a free domain on hosting plans |

<Tip>
Annual plans save you money compared to monthly billing. You also get a free domain registration included with annual Web Hosting, WordPress Hosting, and Reseller Hosting plans.
</Tip>

## Invoices

Invoices are generated automatically before each renewal date. You receive an email notification when a new invoice is created.

To view your invoices:

1. Log in to the [client area](https://my.speedypage.com)
2. Click **Billing** → **My Invoices**
3. Select an invoice to view details or download a PDF

## Manage payment methods

To add, update, or remove a payment method:

1. Log in to the [client area](https://my.speedypage.com)
2. Go to **Billing** → **Payment Methods**
3. Add a new card or PayPal account, or remove an existing one

<Note>
If a payment fails, you receive an email notification with a link to pay the outstanding invoice manually. Services may be suspended if invoices remain unpaid.
</Note>
