---
title: Cancellations and refunds
description: How to cancel a SpeedyPage service and request a refund under the 30-day money-back guarantee.
---

SpeedyPage offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on hosting plans. There are no long-term contracts — you can cancel any time.

## Cancel a service

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the client area">
    Go to [my.speedypage.com](https://my.speedypage.com) and log in with your account credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your service">
    Click **Services** and select the service you want to cancel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request cancellation">
    Click **Request Cancellation**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a cancellation type">
    Select one of the following:

    - **Immediate** — the service is cancelled and removed right away
    - **End of billing period** — the service remains active until your current billing cycle ends, then it is cancelled
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Submit the cancellation request.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Immediate cancellations permanently delete all files, databases, email accounts, and backups associated with the service. Make sure you have a copy of any data you need before requesting an immediate cancellation.
</Warning>

## 30-day money-back guarantee

If you cancel a hosting plan within 30 days of your initial purchase, you can request a full refund. This applies to:

- Web Hosting
- WordPress Hosting
- Reseller Hosting
- Virtual Servers (NVMe VPS)

Refund requests within the 30-day window are processed to your original payment method. Processing time depends on your payment provider but is typically 5-10 business days.

## What is not refundable

- **Domain registrations** — domains are non-refundable, including free domains received with annual hosting plans
- **Services past the 30-day window** — refunds are not available after the initial 30-day period

<Note>
If you received a free domain with an annual hosting plan and request a refund within 30 days, the cost of the domain registration is deducted from the refund amount.
</Note>

## After cancellation

- **End of billing period cancellations** — your service continues to work until the current cycle ends. No further invoices are generated.
- **Immediate cancellations** — all data is removed. This cannot be reversed.

If you change your mind before a scheduled cancellation takes effect, you can revoke it from the same service page in the client area.
