---
title: Upgrade or downgrade your plan
description: How to change your SpeedyPage hosting plan through the client area.
---

You can upgrade or downgrade your hosting plan at any time through the client area. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

## Change your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the client area">
    Go to [my.speedypage.com](https://my.speedypage.com) and log in with your account credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your service">
    Click **Services** and select the hosting plan you want to change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Upgrade/Downgrade">
    Click the **Upgrade/Downgrade** button. You'll see a list of available plans and the price difference.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a new plan">
    Select the plan you want to move to. The page shows the prorated cost or credit for the change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the change">
    Review the summary and confirm. For upgrades, the prorated charge is billed immediately. For downgrades, the change takes effect at your next renewal date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How billing works for plan changes

- **Upgrades** — you're charged the prorated difference between your current plan and the new plan for the remainder of your billing cycle. The upgrade applies immediately.
- **Downgrades** — no immediate charge or refund. Your current plan remains active until the end of the billing period, and the new plan takes effect at renewal.

<Note>
VPS plan changes may require a different process depending on the resource adjustments needed. [Contact support](https://speedypage.com/contact) to discuss VPS upgrades or downgrades.
</Note>
